WHO AM I

Who Am I? a question we all ask ourselves at least once in our lifetime or keep questioning as we go through life. The evaluative process of one’s persona is a never-ending process. We always have a vivid perception of what we think of ourselves which could be derived from others’ perception of us.

I think for one to understand themselves or get to a point of self-actualization we tend to borrow and compare certain attributes that we have observed from others mostly those older than us.
