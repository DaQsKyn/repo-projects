Project Scope

Reasons for Carrying out the project

The sky is not the limit for this company, with their new and well-designed fashion ideas it has the potential to reach greater heights. Mr. Hasham decided that the only way to cope with increasingly competitive market is to come up with innovative ways to put his company’s name out there. He decided to hire me as the project manager so that I could creative and publish a multimedia project that is suitable for this company. “Something fun and exciting for our customers’ to enjoy using and that will pass on information that will inform them on what they need to know about the company”, is what Mr. Hasham described as a successful multimedia project. My love for fashion drew me to accepting to help Mr. Hasham with this project.

Benefits to the Company

The following are the benefits to the company:

-> Any updates on the company information can be altered to keep the customers up to date with any offers.

->Larger market, both nationally and internationally. More people will get to know about the brand.

->With the project successfully taking off there will be a great increase in sales, thus increasing their profits.

->With their increased profit margins, the business will be able to expand their business and have more branches.

->A larger base of customers will be acquired.

->It will save on costly methodsof advertising for example newspaper articles and radio airtime.

What the product will communicate

• A brief introduction of the company and the services it offers.

• New stock and special offers on item being sold.

• Contact details and location of the company.

• Pictures of new designs. We are going to communicate to potential customers about upcoming events.

Project Aims

I shall outline the objectives and aims that this project shall be expected to deliver by the end of the timeline:

Should be able to attract more customers.

There should be an evident increase in the company’s profits.

The customers should be able to provide feedback about the company’s products.

Information on the company should be clearly displayed.

This will be used as a marketing tool for the company which will in turn increase the profits.

Constraints

Time- The client along with the other stakeholders agreed upon deadlines. The project should be done before the dates’ set, it is my duty as the project manager to ensure that the project is done on time if not then it shall be considered a failure.

Finances- The client and the Chief financing officer are the ones in-charge of providing the finances, which means there will be limited funds. Therefore, the funds need to be utilized wisely and unnecessary expenditure should be avoided.

Resources- The material resources and human labour set aside for the project are limited, which means that they need to be used efficiently.

The Project will be considered successful when:

-The completion of the project meets the deadline.

-The project does not go over the budget, stays within the budget.

-All resources provided are used efficiently and not wasted.

-A complete multimedia product is handed to the stakeholders.

-All milestones must be achieved.

Areas of Risk

Unavailability of team members – Some things cannot be avoided for example- sickness -, if any of the team members fall ill, this will cause a drag to the project and therefore not meeting our deadlines.

Solution: Come up with a plan that will ensure flexibility for all of the team members.

Client’s absence – If there is any event where the client is not able to make it to the review meetings, there could be a miscommunication of his vision of the project.

Solution: The client should pass on the information of his ideas beforehand to avoid any inconvenience, or to conduct informal meetings with the project manager.

Malfunctioning of equipment – Malfunctioning of the equipment is something that will cause great drag on the project, some of the systems could crash or some of the storage hardware could be misplaced.

Solution: One solution is that we could talk to the school to allow us to have the IT technician on site with us in-case of any system failure.

Virus infection/accidental loss of work – A virus, or loss of work is something that could easily happen with such a big project, it will corrupt the system which might also lead to the product and all its files being deleted which will be detrimental to this project.

Solution: In the case of this happening we shall take great prevention measures to ensure the safety of our work, by installing the latest and best functioning anti-viruses in all computers and have a backup system in place.
